About Us

Devin is a hilariously tall software engineer whose powerful love for all birds is both heartwarming and unnerving. Their hobbies include picking locks, piecing together the rules to heavy Euro games, and composing lists of un-Googlable DJ names. Devin's dream is to move to the sky, where birds live.
Sara is a hilariously short English professor who writes a lot of stories about corpses, both walking and not. In her spare time, she reads about disasters and collects funny brochures from hotels and airports. Her favorite war is the Tripolitan War, and she is baffled as to why a war between Thomas Jefferson and pirates gets so little attention.
Devin and Sara met in 2012 and spent six months trying to figure out whether or not they were dating. Seven years, three apartments, and one cross-country move later, it seems like the answer to that question was probably "yes." They live in Chicago, with their four current birds and various objects for those birds to sit on.
